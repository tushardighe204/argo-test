My Music Stats

  • Quickly & comprehensively visualise your music data.
  • Discover interesting information about your listening habits.
  • No data collected or saved.
  • Open source with no ads.
  • Can be installed as a PWA (Progressive Web App).
  • Pure JavaScript & HTML. No backend server involved.
  • Superfast & lightweight.
  • Fully responsive.
  • Looks great on mobile devices.
  • Beautiful card view with delightful animations.
  • Interactive & responsive charts.
  • Unique-O-Meter - See how unique your music taste is.

    • Welcome to My Music Stats!

    Visualise your Last.fm data, analyse your music habits. See how unique your music taste is.

    Open source & ad free

    To check your stats, enter your Last.fm username/ID in the textbox above & press Submit. To see my stats, enter my ID shashank-ssriva. You can also enter any Last.fm username to check their stats.

    This application can also be installed as a PWA (Progressive Web App).
